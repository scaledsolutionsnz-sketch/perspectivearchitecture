About Clint Gaby Perspective Architecture Limited

A Reliable Partner
for Every Build

Established by Clint Gaby, Perspective Architecture Limited provides clients with a reliable, cost-effective architectural design and draughting service from our base in Mosgiel, Dunedin.

With over 20 years of experience across architectural, electrical engineering, and building services consultancy throughout New Zealand, Clint brings a comprehensive understanding of construction co-ordination and documentation to every project.

Licensed Building Practitioner (Design 1)
Diplomas in Architectural Draughting & Architectural Technology
Specialist in AutoCAD & Revit CAD software
Member of ADNZ (Architectural Designers NZ)
Serving Dunedin, Mosgiel, Invercargill & surrounds
What We Do

Our Services

Comprehensive architectural design solutions tailored to your project, timeline, and budget.

01
Residential Design

New homes, additions, alterations and renovations. We design spaces that reflect your personality, lifestyle, and the way you live — from concept through to consent.

02
Commercial Design

Commercial and light-industrial projects designed with a practical eye for function, compliance, and cost-effectiveness without compromising on quality.

03
Contract Draughting

High-quality construction documentation using AutoCAD and Revit. Clear, precise drawings that communicate intent to builders, engineers, and councils.

04
Building Consents

Thorough, well-prepared consent documentation that gives your application the best chance of a smooth, efficient approval process through your local council.

05
Additions & Alterations

Transform your existing home or commercial space. We assess what you have and design solutions that integrate seamlessly with the existing structure.

06
Garages & Outbuildings

From triple car garages to sleepouts, minor dwellings, and farm buildings. Practical, well-detailed structures that add real value to your property.

How We Work

Our Process

01
Initial Consultation

We meet on-site or via phone to understand your brief, vision, budget, and timeline in full detail.

02
Concept Design

We develop initial design ideas and floor plan options, refining them with your feedback until the design feels right.

03
Documentation

Full construction documentation using AutoCAD and Revit — clear, coordinated, and ready for council submission.

04
Consent & Build

We prepare and lodge your building consent application and support your builder throughout the construction phase.
